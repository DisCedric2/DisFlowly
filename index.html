Flowly can automate virtually any repetitive business process. Common use cases include lead management, customer onboarding, invoice processing, employee onboarding, marketing campaign automation, data synchronization between apps, and much more. Our platform is flexible enough to handle simple if-this-then-that rules as well as complex multi-step workflows.

No coding skills are required! Flowly's visual workflow builder allows you to create automations through an intuitive drag-and-drop interface. For advanced users, we do offer the ability to add custom JavaScript code at any step in your workflow, but this is completely optional.

Most customers can get started with basic automations in under 30 minutes. Connecting your apps is quick and easy with our guided setup process. More complex workflows might take a few hours to design and test. Our customer success team is available to help with onboarding if needed.
